# University Learning Workflow

This is parked for later, but kept here so it does not disappear.

## Goal

Make university learning less reactive by preparing the material before the semester or week starts.

## Before A Module Starts

Download all required PDFs, slides, readings, assignment briefs, and module outlines.

Create a module folder with:

- `outline.md`
- `readings/`
- `assignments/`
- `weekly-notes/`
- `questions.md`

## For Each Week

Read the weekly material once quickly to see the shape.

Write:

- What this week is about.
- What terms must be known.
- What procedures must be practiced.
- What examples should be solved again.
- What questions still feel unclear.

## Testing Understanding

After reading, make a short self-test:

- Definitions to recall.
- One easy worked example.
- One medium worked example.
- One explanation question.
- One "why does this work?" question.

## Keep It Small

The weekly goal is not to master everything immediately.

The goal is to avoid being surprised by the material, then steadily turn confusion into examples, questions, and finished notes.
